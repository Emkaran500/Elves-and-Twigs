THE RULES

- Players pick one out of the four factions as a starting point.

- The players must either take a sheet of paper for tracking resources or a Game Companion (Refer to Materials required).

- The turn order is clockwise from the starting player, everybody rolls a die, and the person with the highest roll starts first, and then goes clockwise.

- Each zone has 25 places, and players move accordingly to a dice roll plus a modifier (Gained from cards and events on the board or items in a shop).

- The player can or cannot add a modifier to the roll.

- Players can buy items from the Item Shop only when they stand in the Merchant Zone.

- The Token Shop is available to the players at all times.
